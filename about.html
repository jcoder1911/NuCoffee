About Us

We are a company from a humble beginnings. We were founded by our great grandfather John Geiger, fondly called John the coffee man by his friends. He believed that great coffee shouldn't just be accessible to the super wealthy. He wanted craft coffee to become widely distributed to everyone at a reasonable price. So he set out to find the best coffees in the world by traveling the jungles of the Amazon and central Africa. You can rest assured that our coffee will give you the "pep" in your step while being all natural and filled with quality.
